Makeup Artist

Bridal Makeup

At Feather Touch, we specialize in creating stunning bridal looks that enhance your natural beauty and suit your personal style. Our expert makeup artists use high-quality products and the latest techniques to ensure your makeup stays flawless throughout your special day. From traditional to contemporary styles, we cater to all bridal makeup needs, making you look radiant and elegant as you walk down the aisle.

Saree Box Folding

Our saree box folding service ensures that your saree is neatly and beautifully folded, making it easy to carry and store. This traditional method of folding not only preserves the saree's fabric and embroidery but also adds a touch of sophistication to your bridal trousseau. Let us handle the intricate details so you can focus on enjoying your special moments.

Bridal Flowers

We provide exquisite bridal flowers that complement your wedding attire. From floral arrangements for your hair to beautiful bouquets, our skilled florists create designs that match your style and theme. Using fresh, vibrant flowers, we ensure that you look enchanting and your floral accessories add to the overall charm of your bridal look.

Rental Jewellery

Feather Touch offers a wide range of luxurious jewellery available for rent, perfect for brides who want to adorn themselves with stunning pieces without the permanent investment. Our collection includes traditional and contemporary designs, ensuring you find the perfect jewellery to match your wedding outfit and style, adding a touch of glamour to your special day.

Professional Mehndi

Our professional mehndi artists create intricate and beautiful mehndi designs that adorn your hands and feet. Using high-quality henna, we ensure that your mehndi is rich in color and lasts long. Whether you prefer traditional patterns or modern designs, our artists will work with you to create the perfect look for your wedding or special occasion.

Nail Art

Enhance your bridal look with our creative and stylish nail art services. Our nail artists use high-quality products and innovative techniques to create designs that complement your wedding attire. From simple elegance to elaborate patterns, we ensure your nails are a perfect match for your special day.

Plate Decoration

Our plate decoration service adds a touch of elegance to your ceremonial plates. Whether it's for a wedding, engagement, or any other special occasion, we create beautifully decorated plates that enhance the aesthetic appeal of your event. Using a variety of materials and designs, we ensure each plate is a work of art.

Model Shoot

Feather Touch provides professional makeup and styling services for model shoots. Our makeup artists work closely with photographers and models to create stunning looks that enhance the visual appeal of the photos. Whether it's for fashion, beauty, or commercial shoots, we ensure you look your best on camera.

Thamboolam Bag

Our Thamboolam bag service offers specially crafted bags that are perfect for your Thamboolam needs. These bags are designed with attention to detail and can be customized to match the theme of your event. Ideal for weddings and special occasions, our Thamboolam bags add a touch of tradition and elegance to your celebrations.
